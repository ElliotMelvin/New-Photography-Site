About Me

Who?

Hi! I’m Elliot and I’m a photographer in the quiet town of Stockport. My favourite things in the world are exploring the world and shopping, and when I'm not taking photographs you'll find me playing video games at home. My love of art is what made me want to be a photographer. I've just started in the indusrty of photography and I'm enjoying it.

Learning?

Since being on a Digital and Technology university course I've learnt how to code with Java, HTML, CSS and Javascript. Coding can be a lot of fun allowing you to create anything you like with endless possibilities. Being on this course has given me new skills, allowing me to create a site like this one. I've faced many challenges.

What I enjoy?

I'm a video game addict, my favourite games are League of Legends, Guild Wars 2, Stardew Valley, Ark Survival, Subnautica and Shadow of Mordor. These are currently my favourite games!
